Before your first nail appointment, bring a few reference photos that show the shape, color, and mood you like.

Tell your nail artist about your daily routine, preferred length, and whether you want a natural or statement finish. Clear details help create a result that feels right for you.

After the appointment, use cuticle oil regularly and avoid using your nail tips as tools. Gentle care keeps the finish looking fresh longer.
